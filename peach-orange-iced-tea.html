Peach Orange Iced Tea Recipe

Description

This is a peach- and orange-flavored iced tea, great on a hot summer day. A nectarine can be substituted for the peach. A mandarin orange can be substituted for the clementine.

Ingredients

  • 1 large fresh peach, sliced
  • 1 clementine, peeled and segmented
  • 1 tablespoom white sugar, or to taste
  • 8 cups boiling water
  • 4 Earl Grey tea bags

Steps

  1. Place peach, clementine, and sugar in a pitcher
  2. Mash the fruit using a spoon
  3. Add water and tea bags and stir
  4. Refrigerate until cool, about 1 hour
  5. Remove fruit and tea bags using a slotted spoon
